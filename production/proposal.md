# Proposal — "How disc golf works" (60 s concept explainer)

- **Shape:** concept explainer (hook → context → 3 how-it-works steps →
  what it means → recap).
- **Audience:** general; someone who has seen a basket in a park and
  wondered.
- **Duration target:** ~60 s narration-led; ~65 s total with breathing room.
- **Theme:** explainer-video design-system light tokens (ground #f5f7fa,
  ink #1a2733, single accent #0078d4), "Segoe UI", system-ui sans.
  Components from the design system: title/hook card layout, step chips,
  flow-diagram SVG grammar, recap card with verb chips.
- **Music:** none — narration only (concept explainer, calm pacing).
- **Scenes:**
  1. Hook — title card: "How does a flying disc become a round of golf?"
  2. Context — stat scene: 9,800+ courses worldwide; played like golf,
     every shot a throw.
  3. Step 01 Tee to basket — flight-path diagram: tee pad → drawn flight
     arc → chain basket.
  4. Step 02 The lie — landed disc becomes the next throwing spot;
     successive arcs.
  5. Step 03 The discs — three rim profiles: driver / mid-range / putter.
  6. What it means — scoring: count every throw; par 3; 9 or 18 holes.
  7. Recap — TEE → LIE → BASKET chips; putter + mid-range starter note.
